Help:

What is a .kml and how do I get it from Google Earth?

A kml file is a Google Earth file that is readable as a text and therefore can be manipulated into new desired document. You can obtain the kml by right clicking on anything (e.g. folder, shape or path) and selecting Save Places As... and picking .kml format


Can't real .kml in Google Earth?

If you've tried double clicking on the file and it opens a different app, you will need to manually choose what application that opens. You can do this by Right clicking on the file, selecting Open with... and select Google Earth


Missing File?

I am aware that this happens it is caused by the fact you have two files in different places that are exactly the same name therefore will overwrite the other one. Contact me for further assistant in the contact tab.
